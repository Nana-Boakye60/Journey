Chapter 2: Blossoming Friendship

Over the weeks, Gillies found excuses to talk to Danielle. They shared stories during breaks, walked home together, and discovered common dreams. Danielle loved drawing and dreamed of becoming an architect. Gillies adored science and wanted to invent something that could change lives. But their growing bond didn’t go unnoticed. Marcus, a popular and confident boy, began hovering around Danielle. He carried her books, cracked jokes, and invited her to join his group. Gillies felt jealousy gnaw at him, unsure if Danielle saw him the same way he saw her.
