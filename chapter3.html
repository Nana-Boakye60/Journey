Chapter 3: The Talent Show (Climax)

The annual talent show arrived. Gillies prepared a science experiment, Danielle readied her artwork, and Marcus planned a bold move — a song dedicated to Danielle. On stage, Marcus sang passionately, and the crowd erupted in cheers. Danielle blushed, and Gillies felt crushed. His experiment suddenly seemed small compared to Marcus’s grand gesture. But backstage, Danielle handed Gillies a sketch. It was him, standing proudly beside his experiment. She whispered, “Marcus is charming, but you inspire me. You make me believe I can chase my dreams.” Gillies realized then that sincerity mattered more than showmanship.
